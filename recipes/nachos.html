Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Paste
  • Water
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Corn Chips

Steps

  1. Cook beef in a skillet over high heat.
  2. Once browned, add tomato paste and water. Cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Preheat oven to 180C, spread corn chips in a single layer in tray
  4. Spread beef mixture over chips and top with cheddar cheese.
  5. Bake in over for 15 minutes.
