Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Panko Breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Cut breast in half horizontally
  2. Use roller to thin meat
  3. Season with salt and pepper, then dip and cover in eggs
  4. Coat meat in flour, shake off excess, dip back into egg and shake of excess.
  5. Press coated meat into panko breadcrumbs
  6. Heat oil in pan and fry until golden brown
